# Dukaan Dosth (దుకాణ్ దోస్త్) — Project Presentation 🚀

## 1. Problem Statement: The Paper Notebook Era
*   **The Problem**: 63M+ small shop owners in India still use paper bahi-khatas.
*   **Pain Points**: Manual math errors, lost credit (udhaar) records, zero visibility into business health.
*   **The Barrier**: Tech intimidation and language barriers. Existing apps feel too "English" or "Complex".

## 2. Our Solution: "Aapka dukaan ka dosth, AI ke saath"
*   **Voice-First**: Just tap and speak (Telugu/English/Tenglish).
*   **AI-Powered Accounting**: Google Gemini parses "Aaj 500 ka rice becha" into structured data.
*   **Daily AI Summary**: Warm, conversational end-of-day reports in the owner's language.
*   **Visual Insights**: Simple profit/loss charts at a glance.

## 3. Tech Stack: Modern & Scalable
*   **Frontend**: React (Vite) — Mobile-first, responsive UI.
*   **Backend**: Spring Boot 3 (Java) — Robust auth, transaction management.
*   **AI Service**: Python FastAPI — Dedicated Gemini API wrapper for parsing and insights.
*   **Database**: Supabase (PostgreSQL) — Cloud-managed, reliable persistence.
*   **AI Model**: Google Gemini 2.0 Flash — Best-in-class multilingual support.

## 4. Key Demo Features (The "Wow" Factors)
1.  **Voice Entry**: Speak a mixed-language sentence and see it parse instantly.
2.  **Transaction Confirmation**: Edit AI's "understanding" before saving.
3.  **End-of-Day Summary**: See the AI "translate" a list of sales into a friendly Telugu message.
4.  **Weekly Health**: A simple green/red bar chart showing if the shop is making money.

## 5. Potential Impact & Future Roadmap
*   **Impact**: Financial literacy and transparency for millions of micro-entrepreneurs.
*   **Next Steps**: 
    - PWA with offline support for patchy internet.
    - WhatsApp integration for summary alerts.
    - Bulk inventory management with restock reminders.
